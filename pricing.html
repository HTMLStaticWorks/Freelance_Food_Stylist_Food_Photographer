Pricing Frequently Asked Questions

What expenses are generally excluded from day rates?

+
Standard exclusions cover ingredient sourcing costs, specialized background boards custom-manufactured for the shoot, model fees, travel costs, and studio kitchen rental space if we shoot outside our New York location.

Do you offer discounts for multi-day cookbook bookings?

+
Yes. Cookbooks or large menu shoots requiring 4 or more consecutive shooting days qualify for custom block packages. We outline these options in the pricing brochure.

What are the licensing rights included in commercial packages?

+
Our standard commercial tier includes perpetual rights for online digital marketing, social feeds, and local print placements. Global billboards and packaging covers require custom licensing quotes.
