Case Study 01

Tuscan Groves Branding Campaign

Goal: Launch a luxury extra-virgin olive oil focusing on raw Mediterranean heritage.

Styling Process: To emphasize viscosity, we back-lit the bottles with a soft diffuse gold reflector. Organic rosemary, fresh garlic bulbs, and sourdough bread crusts were arranged around the bottle on custom-aged timber planks to project raw authenticity.

Result: Delivered 12 high-resolution billboard-grade campaign captures used globally. Online brand inquiries increased by 40% in Q1.

Case Study 02

Nectar Distillers Citrus Gin

Goal: Capture botanical freshness and liquid splash dynamics using strobe synchronization.

Styling Process: Custom clear ice blocks were chipped to fit hand-blown crystal tumblers. Gin pours were captured at 1/8000s strobe speed to freeze droplets mid-air against a textured slate backdrop, establishing beautiful highlights.

Result: Provided 8 premium social assets and magazine cover spreads, leading to a sold-out summer release within three weeks.
