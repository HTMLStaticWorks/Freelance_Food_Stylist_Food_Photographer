Styling Expertise

The Art of Food Styling

Food styling is more than just placing ingredients. It is about understanding the organic chemistry of food under high-intensity photography lights. We hand-select props, curate fabrics, and use specialized food preservation techniques to ensure every ingredient looks raw, appetizing, and editorial-grade.

Methodology

How We Work Together

01

Concept & Moodboard Development

We start by discussing your brand guidelines and generating styling moodboards. We align on color grading, plating props, backgrounds, and specific visual composition rules.

02

Sourcing & Preparation

Elena hand-picks organic ingredients, herbs, and unique props from local farms and specialized culinary merchants. Prep lists are set for optimal freshness on shooting day.

03

Styling, Plating & Lighting

In the kitchen studio, ingredients are prepped and styled. Lights are configured to mimic soft natural window sunlight, establishing soft highlights and premium shadows.

04

The Capture & Post-Production

Using medium format GFX cameras, plates are shot. Files are checked live on monitors. High-end color grading and retouching preserve food natural realism without looking artificial.
